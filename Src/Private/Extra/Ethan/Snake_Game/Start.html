How To Play

Start by moving the dot using the up, down, left, and right arrow keys on the keyboard, or W, A, S, and D. Correct answers will grow the dot into a snake. Keep moving the dot or snake, and prevent it from hitting itself or the walls. Hitting the red apples and answering the question correctly will grow your snake, however an incorrect answer will remove a heart. To answer a question, move your snake to an apple, and then click with the computer mouse. Each correct answer produces a regeneration point. After 4 regeneration points, an extra heart will be awarded, or bonus points if you have all 5 hearts already. The goal of the game is to answer 25 questions correctly. The scoreboard on the top left of the game screen tracks your current score, the goal score, and your highest score. The space bar is used to pause the game. The escape button will close the game at any point, including the start page. Click on the settings image at the top right of the screen to add variety to the game, if desired. Best of luck!

  • Up Arrow: Moves Snake Up

  • Left Arrow: Moves Snake Left

  • Down Arrow: Moves Snake Down

  • Right Arrow: Moves Snake Right
  • Space Bar: Pauses Game






  • Escape Button: Quits Game by Closing Tab
