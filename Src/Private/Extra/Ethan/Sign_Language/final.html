How To Use

Simply type in the sentence below without any punctuation or numbers. Only insert letters into the converter. Once the sentence has been inserted, press enter and click out of the area. The starting image will pop up. From that point, click on the left and right arrows around the picture to increment the sequence by one forward or backward. The other option is to press the play button below the image. This will move the sequence forward on a timer. Move the slider bar to the left to slow the process down, or to the right to speed it up.

This translator only supports letters. The translator spells out the phrase given to it in sign language and does not recognize words.
